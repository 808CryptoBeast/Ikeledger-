Wallet and seed phrase rules
  • Write seed phrases offline and store them privately. Never photograph, screenshot, paste, email, or DM them.
  • Never enter a seed phrase into a website. Xaman approvals should happen inside Xaman, not inside a browser form.
  • Separate daily-use wallets from long-term holdings and test recovery with small amounts before relying on it.
Signing checklist
  • Review network, destination, amount, asset issuer, destination tag, memo, and fee before approving.
  • Reject blind signatures, surprise permission requests, rushed approvals, and transactions you did not start.
  • Refresh account state after signing so you can confirm the ledger outcome.
Project red flags
  • Guaranteed returns, urgent countdowns, copied roadmaps, anonymous teams, or pressure to buy immediately.
  • Locked withdrawals, unclear token supply, hidden admin controls, vague issuer details, or no verifiable history.
  • Support DMs, fake airdrops, wallet-drain links, and "verify your wallet" forms.
Market discipline
  • Check liquidity, volume, issuer reputation, spreads, and exit paths before trading.
  • Use small test transactions with new issuers, bridges, exchanges, or unfamiliar assets.
  • Avoid chasing pumps, influencer signals, and charts without fundamentals.

Account Overview

XRPL Reserve System

Base Reserve: 1 XRP (lowered Dec 2024)

Owner Reserve: 0.2 XRP per ledger object

Objects counted: Trust lines, NFTs, DEX offers, escrows, payment channels, checks

Why reserves? Prevents ledger spam and keeps accounts viable on-chain.

Owner reserve is returned when the object is deleted. Base reserve is only recovered by deleting the account.

    How Key Generation Works
    
                
    
                  
    Ed25519 keypair — generated using your browser's built-in crypto.subtle.generateKey API. No external library, no network requests, no servers involved.
    
                  
    XRPL address derivation: SHA-256 → RIPEMD-160 of your public key, then Base58Check encoded with a version byte of 0x00. Same algorithm the XRPL uses natively.
    
                  
    Private key format: 64-character hex (32 raw bytes) — compatible with xrpl.js and other XRPL tools.
    
                  
    Keys are held only in browser memory and cleared the moment you leave this page.
    
                
    
              
    
              
    
                
    XRPL Reserve Requirements
    
                
    
                  
    Base reserve: 1 XRP — required to activate any new XRPL account. Cannot be spent; only recovered by deleting the account.
    
                  
    Owner reserve: 0.2 XRP per ledger object you own (trust lines, NFTs, DEX offers, escrows, payment channels).
    
                  
    Recommended first deposit: Send at least 2 XRP — 1 XRP reserve + 1 XRP you can actually spend.
    
                  
    Reserves were lowered from 10 XRP / 2 XRP in December 2024 via validator vote.
    
                
    
              
    
              
    
                
    Security Best Practices
    
                
    
                  
    Write it down: Paper and pen, stored offline. Fireproof safe or safety deposit box are ideal locations.
    
                  
    No screenshots: Many phones auto-back up photos to the cloud — this can expose your key.
    
                  
    No password managers for keys: Keep your XRPL private key separate from your regular passwords.
    
                  
    Use Xaman to sign: Import your address into Xaman — never paste your private key into websites.
    
                  
    No legitimate support team, wallet app, or project will ever ask for your private key.
